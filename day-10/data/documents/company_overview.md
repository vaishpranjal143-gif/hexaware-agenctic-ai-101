# Caramel AI — Company Overview

Caramel AI is a fictional enterprise software company founded in 2021 that builds
AI-powered customer support assistants for mid-size businesses. The company is
headquartered in Bengaluru, India, with a remote-first engineering team spread
across 12 countries.

Caramel AI's flagship product is the Caramel Assistant, a chat-based support
agent that plugs into a company's existing help center, ticketing system, and
product documentation. The assistant answers customer questions instantly by
retrieving relevant information from the company's own knowledge base rather
than relying purely on a general-purpose language model's memory.

As of 2026, Caramel AI serves over 400 business customers across retail,
fintech, and SaaS verticals, processing roughly 2 million support
conversations per month. The company's mission is "to make every company's
knowledge instantly answerable."

Caramel AI was named one of the top 50 AI startups to watch by TechRadar in
2025 and raised a $22 million Series A round led by Northbridge Ventures in
early 2026.
