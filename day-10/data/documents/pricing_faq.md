# Caramel Assistant — Pricing FAQ

**What pricing plans are available?**
Caramel AI offers three plans: Starter ($299/month, up to 1,000
conversations), Growth ($899/month, up to 10,000 conversations), and
Enterprise (custom pricing, unlimited conversations with dedicated support).

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. All new customers get a 14-day free trial of the Growth plan with no
credit card required.

**What counts as a "conversation"?**
A conversation is a single customer chat session, regardless of how many
messages are exchanged within it. Sessions that are idle for more than 30
minutes start a new conversation if the customer returns.

**Do you charge extra for human handoff?**
No. Human handoff routing is included in all plans. However, the Starter plan
does not include the analytics dashboard's knowledge-gap report.

**Can I switch plans mid-cycle?**
Yes, you can upgrade at any time and the new plan takes effect immediately
with a prorated charge. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next
billing cycle.

**What is the refund policy?**
Caramel AI offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on the first billing cycle
for annual Enterprise contracts. Monthly plans are non-refundable once a
billing cycle has started.
