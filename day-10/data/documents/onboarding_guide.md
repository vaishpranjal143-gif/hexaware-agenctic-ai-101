# Caramel Assistant — Onboarding Guide

Setting up the Caramel Assistant for a new customer takes four steps and
typically completes within one business day.

**Step 1: Connect your knowledge sources.** From the admin dashboard, connect
your help center (Zendesk Guide, Intercom Articles, or a custom URL sitemap),
and optionally upload PDF manuals or CSV files of past support tickets.

**Step 2: Configure the assistant's persona.** Set the assistant's name, tone
(formal, friendly, or concise), and supported languages. You can also upload
a brand logo and set primary/secondary brand colors for the chat widget.

**Step 3: Set escalation rules.** Define the confidence threshold for human
handoff, business hours for live agent availability, and which topics (e.g.
billing disputes, account cancellations) should always route to a human
regardless of confidence score.

**Step 4: Test and go live.** Use the built-in test console to ask sample
questions and review the retrieved sources and generated answers before
embedding the chat widget on your website or connecting it to Slack.

After go-live, Caramel AI recommends reviewing the "knowledge gaps" report
weekly for the first month to identify missing documentation, since answer
quality depends directly on the completeness of the connected knowledge
sources.
