# Account & Loyalty FAQ

## NimbusPlus membership

NimbusPlus (₹499/year) includes free Express shipping on all orders, early access to
sales, and an extended 45-day return window instead of the standard 30 days.

## Loyalty points

Every order earns 2 NimbusPoints per ₹100 spent. Points are credited 10 days after
delivery (to allow for the return window) and are redeemable at checkout, 100 points =
₹10. Points expire 12 months after being earned if unused.

## Changing your email or phone number

Email and phone number can be changed from Account → Profile. A verification code is
sent to the **new** email/phone before the change takes effect, to prevent account
takeover.

## Forgotten password

Password resets are self-service via "Forgot password" on the login page and do not
require contacting support. Support agents can never reset a password directly or ask
for your current password.

## Multiple accounts

NimbusMart allows one account per email address but does not restrict how many
accounts a person has under different emails; loyalty points and NimbusPlus benefits
do not transfer between accounts.
