# Troubleshooting Guide

## Laptop won't turn on

1. Hold the power button for 15 seconds to force a full shutdown, then power on again.
2. Plug directly into a wall outlet (not a power strip) for at least 30 minutes, as a
   fully drained battery can prevent the power button from responding immediately.
3. If a charging LED never lights up with the original charger, the unit likely
   qualifies for an in-warranty repair — file a claim via "Request Repair."

## Phone battery draining quickly after an update

This is commonly caused by background app re-indexing right after a software update
and typically resolves within 24-48 hours. If drain continues past 48 hours, check
Settings → Battery → Usage for a single app consuming an unusually high share, and
reinstall that app.

## Earbuds won't pair

1. Ensure the earbuds are charged (case LED should be white, not red).
2. Forget the device in your phone's Bluetooth settings, then reset the earbuds by
   holding the case button for 10 seconds until the LED flashes.
3. Re-pair as a new device. If pairing still fails, this is a hardware fault covered
   under the 6-month earbud warranty (not the non-returnable policy — warranty and
   returns are handled separately).

## TV has no signal from a connected device

1. Confirm the correct HDMI input is selected on the TV remote.
2. Try a different HDMI cable and port — a worn cable is the most common cause.
3. If the issue persists across multiple ports and cables, this is a covered warranty
   repair, not a returnable defect if the TV is outside the return window.

## Smartwatch won't sync with the app

1. Confirm Bluetooth and location permissions are enabled for the NimbusMart Wear app.
2. Restart both the watch (hold the side button 10 seconds) and the phone's Bluetooth.
3. If the watch shows a persistent red exclamation icon after these steps, it
   qualifies for warranty repair.
