# Shipping Policy

## Standard shipping

Orders over **₹999** (or the equivalent in your local currency) ship free via
Standard Delivery, arriving in **3-5 business days**. Orders below that threshold
carry a flat **₹79** shipping fee.

## Express shipping

Express Delivery (**1-2 business days**) is available at checkout for a flat fee of
**₹249**, regardless of order size. Express orders placed before 2 PM local time ship
the same day.

## International shipping

NimbusMart currently ships to India, the United States, the United Kingdom, and the
European Union. International orders take **7-14 business days** and may be subject
to local customs duties, which are the customer's responsibility.

## Order tracking

A tracking link is emailed as soon as a warehouse marks an order "shipped." Tracking
updates can lag the carrier's own system by up to 12 hours — this is normal and not a
sign of a lost package.

## Delayed or lost packages

If a tracking number shows no movement for **5 business days**, contact support and a
replacement or refund will be issued without requiring the original package to be
located first.

## Address changes

Shipping addresses can be changed only while an order status is "processing." Once an
order moves to "shipped," the address can no longer be changed and the customer should
instead arrange redelivery or pickup directly with the carrier.
