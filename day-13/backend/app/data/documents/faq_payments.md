# Payments FAQ

## Accepted payment methods

Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex), UPI, net banking, and NimbusMart
store credit. EMI (installment) plans are available on orders over ₹3,000 from
participating banks, shown at checkout if your card qualifies.

## Why was my payment declined?

The most common reasons are: insufficient funds, the bank's fraud filter flagging a
first-time large purchase, an expired card, or too many failed attempts triggering a
temporary block. NimbusMart does not control bank-side declines — contact your bank
first, then support if the issue persists after 24 hours.

## Multiple currencies

Prices are shown in your local currency at checkout based on shipping address, but
orders are always charged in **Indian Rupees (INR)** to the card, with your bank
applying its own conversion rate. This can cause the amount on your statement to
differ slightly from the price shown at checkout.

## Invoices and GST

A GST-compliant invoice is emailed automatically after every order and is also
downloadable from "My Orders" → "Invoice." Business customers can add a GSTIN to
their account for it to appear on future invoices automatically.

## Failed payment but order shows as placed

Occasionally a payment can appear to fail on your screen but still be captured by the
bank. If this happens, the order will show as "payment pending" for up to 30 minutes
before either confirming automatically or being cancelled with a full refund.
