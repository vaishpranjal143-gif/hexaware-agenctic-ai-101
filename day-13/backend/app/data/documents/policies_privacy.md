# Privacy & Payment Security

## What we store

NimbusMart stores your name, shipping address, order history, and a tokenized
reference to your payment method. We **never** store full card numbers, CVV codes, or
card PINs on our own servers — all card processing is handled by a PCI-DSS-compliant
payment processor.

## Support agents and card data

NimbusMart support staff (human or automated) can see the **last 4 digits** of a card
on file and the card's expiry month/year, but can never see, request, or "look up" a
full card number, CVV, or PIN. If you are ever asked for your full card number by
someone claiming to be NimbusMart support, that is not a legitimate request — please
report it.

## Data retention

Order history is retained for 7 years for tax and warranty-verification purposes.
Browsing and search history used for product recommendations can be cleared any time
from Account → Privacy → Clear activity.

## Account deletion

Accounts can be deleted from Account → Privacy → Delete account. Deletion is
permanent after a 14-day grace period and removes personal data except records legally
required to be kept (e.g. completed order invoices for tax purposes).
