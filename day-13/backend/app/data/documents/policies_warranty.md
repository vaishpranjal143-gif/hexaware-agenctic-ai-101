# Warranty Policy

## Standard coverage

- **Laptops and desktops:** 1-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects.
- **Smartphones and tablets:** 1-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects.
- **Headphones and audio accessories:** 6-month warranty covering hardware defects.
- **Small appliances:** 1-year warranty; large appliances (refrigerators, washing
  machines) carry a **2-year** warranty.

## What's covered

Manufacturing defects, battery capacity dropping below 80% of original within the
warranty period (laptops and phones only), and component failure under normal use.

## What's NOT covered

Accidental damage (drops, liquid spills, cracked screens), unauthorized repairs or
modifications, cosmetic wear, and damage from using non-NimbusMart-approved chargers
or accessories.

## Extended warranty

NimbusMart Care+ can be purchased within 30 days of the original order and extends
coverage to 3 years total on laptops, desktops, smartphones, and tablets, and adds
**one** accidental-damage repair per year.

## Filing a warranty claim

Warranty claims are filed from "My Orders" → "Request Repair." Support will issue a
prepaid shipping label for in-warranty repairs; there is no charge for parts or labor
on covered defects.
