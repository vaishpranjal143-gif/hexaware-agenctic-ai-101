# Returns & Refunds Policy

NimbusMart accepts returns on most items within **30 days** of delivery, provided the
item is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt or
order confirmation email.

## Non-returnable items

The following items cannot be returned once opened, for hygiene and licensing reasons:

- Earbuds and in-ear headphones
- Software license keys and digital gift cards
- Opened consumables (screen protectors, cleaning kits)

## Refund timing

Once a return is received at our warehouse, refunds are processed within **5-7
business days** back to the original payment method. Store-credit refunds (chosen at
checkout) are issued instantly.

## Restocking fee

Large appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, TVs 55" and above) carry a **10%
restocking fee** if returned for reasons other than a manufacturing defect.

## Damaged or wrong item

If an item arrives damaged or is not what you ordered, contact support within **48
hours** of delivery with photos. NimbusMart covers return shipping in full for these
cases and does not apply a restocking fee.

## How to start a return

Returns are initiated from the "My Orders" page by selecting the order and choosing
"Return item." A prepaid shipping label is emailed within one business day of the
request being approved.
